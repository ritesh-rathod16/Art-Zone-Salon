Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book an appointment?

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance for weekday appointments and 1-2 weeks in advance for weekends. For special services like keratin treatments or bridal packages, we suggest booking 2-3 weeks ahead.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a 50% service charge. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

Do you use professional hair products?

Yes, we exclusively use professional-grade products from top brands like Loreal, Schwarzkopf, and Olaplex. Our stylists will recommend products based on your hair type and the service you're receiving.

How long do keratin treatments last?

Our keratin treatments typically last 3-5 months depending on your hair type, how often you wash your hair, and proper aftercare. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to help extend the life of your treatment.

Do you offer bridal packages?

Yes, we offer customized bridal packages that can include hair trials, wedding day styling, makeup services, and bridal party services. Please contact us at least 2 months before your wedding date to discuss your needs.
